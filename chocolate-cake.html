Decadent Chocolate Cake For Homebodies

This recipe features an icing made with cream cheese, cocoa, and cinnamon frosts this dark chocolate cake for an elegant dessert.

This recipe could easily be flavored with coffee, juice, or buttermilk in place of the water. FANTASTIC!
